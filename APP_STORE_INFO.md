# divclok - Smart Time Tracker

## App Store Description

**Transform your productivity with divclok - the elegant time tracker designed for focused work.**

### ✨ Key Features

**🎯 Simple 3-Activity Focus**

- Track up to 3 different activities simultaneously
- Beautiful pie chart visualization of your time allocation
- Seamless switching between activities with a tap

**⏰ Smart Daily Reset**

- Customize your "day start" time (default: 22:00 JST)
- Perfect for night owls and flexible schedules
- Automatic data archiving at your preferred reset time

**📊 Insightful Analytics**

- Monthly calendar view with daily statistics
- Visual pie charts for each day's breakdown
- Track your productivity patterns over time

**🌸 Beautiful Design**

- Clean, minimal interface
- Soothing pastel color palette
- Smooth animations and transitions

**🔒 Privacy First**

- All data stored locally on your device
- No cloud sync, no data collection
- Your time tracking stays private

### Perfect For:

- Students tracking study sessions
- Freelancers managing multiple projects
- Anyone wanting to understand their time usage
- People with non-traditional schedules

### What Makes divclok Special:

Unlike other time trackers, divclok focuses on simplicity and visual clarity. The unique 3-slice approach prevents overwhelming choice paralysis while the customizable reset time accommodates any lifestyle.
