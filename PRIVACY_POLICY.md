# Privacy Policy for divclok

**Last updated:** June 16, 2025

## Information We Collect

divclok is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best time tracking experience:

### Data Stored Locally

- **Time tracking data**: Your daily activity times and statistics
- **Settings**: Your preferred reset time and app preferences
- **Usage patterns**: App usage statistics for performance optimization

### Data We Don't Collect

- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT track your location beyond timezone detection
- We do NOT share your data with third parties
- We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels

## How We Use Your Information

- **Time Tracking**: Store your activity data locally on your device
- **Timezone Detection**: Automatically adjust for JST timezone
- **App Improvement**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve performance

## Data Storage

All your personal data is stored locally on your device using:

- iOS UserDefaults for settings
- Core Data for time tracking statistics
- No cloud storage or external servers

## Data Security

- All data remains on your device
- No data transmission to external servers
- iOS security features protect your information

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Delete the app and all associated data
- Export your data (contact support)
- Request information about data usage

- Email: vngbh@example.com
- App Store: divclok Support

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app or through the App Store.
